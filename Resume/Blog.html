My Holy Vacation

Holy Week, August 03, 2018

A day to Remember! Vacation with God ,family and friend!

Holy Week is a time for meditations and to reflect to our actions and prays. In this week we were about to remember our Lord Jesus Christ who have been crucified died and was buried for the salvation of many. So let us make this week be a wonderful remembrance to everyone.

What I did during the holy week is that I studied starting Monday to Wednesday. I call my mom to leave a money for me also to go the province to have vacation and so she left me a money for my fare going to La Union in Pugo, Brgy.Amabangonan. Luckily my last teacher is very nice who came down from heaven that likes an angel who saved my day dismiss as early. So I was so glad that I will be able to make it, that I will be able to be having a bonding with my family and other relatives in the province. So I went home early as fast as I can to be able to reach the last trip in a via Manaoag bus because it has a shorter line than via Dagupan that has longer line. Finally ,I make it that I reached the last trip even if its standing. By the way its my first time to go to my province alone. And when I was near to the crossing someone laughed in front while we were inside the bus and I was thinking that it was same as my father's laugh but maybe its just my imagination. But when we reached the crossing I went down the bus then I was surprised and shocked that my father came to without even noticing that we were in the same bus. So what I did is he paid the fare in tricycle. When we reached there I was telling my mom what happen and all of them were laughing because I really didn't notice my father was riding the same bus with me. After that we rest, I sleep in our sofa because lot of us were and we cousins were reunited. But some of us didn't make it because they were busy at their jobs.

Thursday, in this day my parents planned that we should go to "karyan" for us ilokano's but in English it is a river. when we reached at the river my brother together with my cousins run fast and they swim while I stayed take a picture because I don't want my skin get darker so I helped my mom and aunt to get "gussipeng" and "bisokol" because meat is not available to us because we were fasting. Then after that we went for a swim a little because I don't want to be exposed. When we were going home we my cousin that we haven't met for how many year she's so beautiful, kind and she's taller now. So when we got home my aunt who is the cousin of my mother invited us again for getting bisokol and gussipeng but in a far place because its for their "pulutan" or food in there table while having some beer. When we were there in the river with my long time no see cousin we were taliking about relationships because they already have not like me my mom didnt allow me to have even one because of study first. Then when night came the foods were washed away because everything has been already eaten because of tired and hunger.
